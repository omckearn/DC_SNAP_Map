Welcome to DC SNAP Retailers

This map helps you find and explore SNAP‑authorized retailers in Washington, DC.

  • Colored emoji markers show store types. Click a marker for details and Directions.
  • Use the filters to narrow by Store Type and Ward. Clear resets; Home recenters on DC.
  • The legend (bottom‑left) shows counts by type and updates with filters.
  • “SNAP Retailers Near Me” lets you enter an address, use your location, or pick on the map to see the nearest retailer for each category with distances and directions.
  • The black outline shows the DC boundary. A blue dot shows your current location if permitted.
